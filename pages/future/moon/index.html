Humans Return to the Moon by the Mid-2020s

In the year 2024, the Moon will no longer be just a memory from Apollo. NASA will have launched the Artemis program—named after Apollo’s sister in mythology—where astronauts will once again set foot on the lunar surface. But this time, it won’t just be for flags and footprints.

Imagine astronauts orbiting the Moon in a high-tech spacecraft called Orion, and a space station circling the Moon as a pit stop for deeper space missions. By 2024, humanity will be preparing to live and work on the Moon as we lay the foundation for our ultimate dream—Mars!
