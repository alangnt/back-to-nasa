Spacecraft Will Search for Life on Icy Moons

By 2024, NASA will send a probe to Jupiter’s mysterious moon, Europa. Scientists in the 1990s already know Europa has a frozen surface, but underneath it could be an ocean bigger than all of Earth’s combined.

Picture this: a spacecraft, the Europa Clipper, flying over Europa dozens of times, using lasers, radar, and futuristic scanners to search for alien life—yes, alien life—beneath the icy crust. This mission won’t just be about photos; it will confirm if Europa is one of our best chances for life beyond Earth.
